Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna is a really delicious Italian dish

It has a lot of calories though!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta sheets
  • Mince
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook mince and tomatoes in a pan
  2. In a glass baking dish add layers of mince, cheese, and pasta sheets
  3. Cook in oven for 30 minutes at 180c
